About me...

Having been raised mostly in Houston Texas, I’m a big fan of the Houston Rockets, rodeo festivals, and of course all the southern seafood the city has to offer. An interesting fact about me is that I’m a big foodie, and during my spare time I enjoy leaving positive Yelp reviews to new food spots that open up around the city, in order to help out any local restaurants in the area. This allows me to be involved with my city’s community in some shape or form, even though miniscule, as it is an important value of mine to leave an influential presence wherever I may be. I am a motivated, trustworthy individual that stays true to the tasks at hand and is always willing to express good energy to others and hopefully make their day a little better.

In terms of education, I am currently pursuing a BS in Computer Science and a minor in Cybersecurity at Texas A&M University. This being my third year at the university, I’ve already been exposed to multiple languages and a variety of projects. However, a majority of these projects primarily focused on just the full functionality, while disregarding what was being visually presented to the user. I wanted to venture off from this aspect of programming and began gravitating towards the more visual functionality and components of projects. With this realization, I sparked my passion of interest through Front End development because it gave me more of a creative outlet to explore different styles of UI/UX for all types of applications. Moving forward, I have been actively building small app projects to continue to learn the ropes of designing applications as well as picking up new languages and libraries that can provide the backbone for an application’s UI. Eventually, I hope that my dedication to advancing in the tech or even professional industry will allow me to make an impact with my skills.
